How our miner works

Quick Overview

When you insert our script at the top of your webpage anyone who visits the page will begin donating their CPU power to perform cryptographic hash calculations which in turn are fed to a mining pool, this is known as Proof-of-Work (POW) mining. Due to high difficulty in mining and large transaction fee, rewards are paid out only and a correct nonce has been found and the threshold has been reached. We are currently working on a GUI for customers to track their rewards.

Coin Type

Currently we mine Monero as we saw it the most feasable coin to be mined within browsers due to its use of the CryptoNight POW algorithm which is suitable for regular computers CPU's unlike Bitcoin which requires extremely expensive and CPU intensive rigs to perform the cryptographic hashing algorithms.

Payout

Due to the high difficulty value in mining Monero and large transaction fees, customers are paid out once the customer has contributed enough hashes towards the mining pool and has built up a respective pending payout of over 0.2 XMR
